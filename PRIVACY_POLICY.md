# KudiManager Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 21, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

KudiManager ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") operates the KudiManager application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. **If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.** If you have questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@kudimanager.com.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly:
- **Account Registration**: Name, email address, password, and business type
- **Business Data**: Sales transactions, expenses, products, inventory, and financial reports
- **Payment Information**: Email and payment plan selection (processed securely via Paystack and Flutterwave)
- **Communication**: Support inquiries and feedback

### Information Collected Automatically:
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
- **Usage Data**: App features accessed, pages viewed, time spent, interactions
- **Cookies & Local Storage**: Session tokens, user preferences, theme settings, language preference
- **Location Data**: None - we do not collect location information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- **Service Provision**: Creating and managing your account, processing transactions
- **Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to improve app functionality and user experience
- **Communication**: Sending account notifications, support responses, and service updates
- **Legal Compliance**: Maintaining records for Nigerian tax and regulatory compliance
- **Security**: Protecting against fraud, unauthorized access, and ensuring data integrity

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- **Encryption**: All data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Storage**: User data is stored securely with access controls and encryption at rest
- **Retention**: Business data is retained while your account is active; deleted 90 days after account closure
- **Payment Data**: Payment information is not stored - processed directly by Paystack and Flutterwave

**Security Measures Implemented:**
- Bcrypt password hashing (10 salt rounds)
- JWT token-based authentication with 7-day expiration
- Rate limiting on authentication endpoints (5 requests/15 minutes)
- CORS protection
- Input validation and SQL injection prevention

## 5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:
- **Paystack** (Nigerian payments) - https://paystack.com/privacy
- **Flutterwave** (International payments) - https://flutterwave.com/privacy
- **Google Gemini AI** (Business advisory) - https://google.com/policies/privacy
- **Google Analytics** (Usage tracking) - https://policies.google.com/privacy

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies before providing data.

## 6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We **do not sell** your personal data. We may share information:
- **With Service Providers**: Payment processors, analytics providers (under data processing agreements)
- **For Legal Reasons**: If required by law, court order, or to protect safety/rights
- **Business Transfer**: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice)

## 7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data (except legally required records)
- **Opt-Out**: Disable cookies and local storage via browser settings
- **Data Portability**: Request data export in a standard format

To exercise these rights, contact: support@kudimanager.com

## 8. Children's Privacy

KudiManager is intended for business owners (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18. If we discover such collection, we will delete it immediately.

## 9. International Users

If you access KudiManager from outside Nigeria, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data internationally. We comply with applicable data protection regulations.

## 10. Cookie & Tracking Policy

**Cookies Used:**
- `kudiUser`: Stores user session data (localStorage)
- `auth_token`: JWT authentication token (localStorage)
- `theme`: User theme preference (localStorage)
- `language`: User language preference (localStorage)

All cookies are optional and can be disabled via browser settings.

## 11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:
- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)

Contact: support@kudimanager.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

## 12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify affected users within 30 days via email and in-app notification with details about the breach and steps to protect your data.

## 13. Changes to Privacy Policy
